Discussion of the Osborne Reynolds Number Experiment

1. What is the theoretical critical Reynolds number of internal flow in pipes? What does this quantity mean?

Answer: The critical Reynolds number should be about 2300. It was determined by gradually increasing the flow rate and observing the transition from laminar to turbulent flow using the dye injection method.
